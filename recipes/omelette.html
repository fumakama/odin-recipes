Omelette

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tablespoon butter or olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Optional fillings such as cheese, ham, mushrooms, onions, or herbs

Instructions:

  1. Crack the eggs into a small bowl and whisk them together until the yolks and whites are fully combined. Season with salt and pepper.
  2. If using fillings, prepare them now by chopping or shredding as needed.
  3. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the butter or olive oil and swirl to coat the pan.
  4. Pour the eggs into the pan and let them cook for 10-15 seconds until they begin to set on the bottom.
  5. Use a spatula to gently push the edges of the omelette towards the center of the pan, tilting the pan to allow the uncooked eggs to flow underneath.
  6. When the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny on top, add any fillings you're using to one side of the omelette.
  7. Use the spatula to carefully fold the other side of the omelette over the fillings, creating a half-moon shape. Slide the omelette onto a plate and serve immediately.

Enjoy your delicious omelette!
